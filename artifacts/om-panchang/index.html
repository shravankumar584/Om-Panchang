Om Panchang – Daily Hindu Panchang & Vedic Calendar

Om Panchang is a free, accurate Hindu Panchang (Panchangam) service providing daily Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana and Vara for hundreds of cities worldwide. The five elements of Panchang — Tithi (lunar day), Nakshatra (lunar mansion), Yoga (auspicious period), Karana (half-day division), and Vara (weekday) — are the backbone of Hindu timekeeping, consulted for every important life event from birth to marriage to business openings.

Every day you can look up Sunrise and Sunset timings, Moonrise and Moonset, Rahu Kalam (inauspicious period ruled by Rahu), Yamaganda Kalam, Gulika Kalam, and Abhijit Muhurta — the most auspicious time of the day. All calculations follow the traditional Vedic system using the Sidereal zodiac (Nirayana) corrected with the Lahiri Ayanamsa.

Om Panchang also offers a full suite of Vedic tools. The Kundali (birth chart) section calculates your Lagna and planetary placements. Kundali Milan (compatibility matching) uses Ashtakoot Guna scoring for marriage compatibility. The Hora calculator shows planetary hours so you can choose the right time for important activities. The Choghadiya calculator divides the day into auspicious and inauspicious slots used widely in Gujarat, Rajasthan and Maharashtra.

Hindu festivals are covered comprehensively — from Diwali, Holi and Navratri to Ekadashi Vrat, Amavasya, Purnima and Pradosh Vrat dates. Monthly Panchang calendars are available for every major Indian city. The Vedic astrology section provides daily horoscope readings for all 12 rashis (zodiac signs), updated every morning with guidance on love, career and health. A growing library of articles covers Hindu deities, mantras, rituals and Vedic concepts in depth.
