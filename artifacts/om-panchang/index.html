What is Panchang?

Panchang (also written Panchanga or Panchangam) literally means "five limbs" in Sanskrit. It is the traditional Hindu almanac that summarises the five key astronomical elements governing each day. Priests, astrologers and devout Hindus consult the Panchang before starting any auspicious activity — choosing a wedding date, naming a newborn, starting a business, or planning a journey. Om Panchang calculates all five elements in real time for your chosen city using precise astronomical algorithms.
